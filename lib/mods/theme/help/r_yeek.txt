#####R=== Yeeks ===

#####GDescription
Yeeks are the least powerful of all the races. They suffer disadvantages 
in nearly all skills and attributes but to compensate they learn (and thus
gain levels) extremely quickly.

Charisma        -5       
Hit Dice Sides  6         
Exp Penalty     -75%
